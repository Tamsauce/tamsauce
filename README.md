# Hi, I'm Tami Hughes 👋
### QA Engineer · Accessibility Specialist · San Diego, CA

I specialize in manual QA, digital accessibility (WCAG 2.2, WAS certified), and shift-left testing strategies that catch issues before they become someone else's emergency. Before moving into QA, I spent years in frontend development, which gives me full-stack context on how products are built and where they tend to fail. 

Outside of QA and Accessibility, I build custom AI agents, automation workflows, and mini SaaS products for mid-market and growing businesses through Tamsauce Consulting.
